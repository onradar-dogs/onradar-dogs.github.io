    National Beagle Day 2026: Celebrate Our Beloved Breed

    What are the two sizes of Beagles?

    Beagles come in two sizes: those under 13 inches at the shoulder and those between 13 and 15 inches.

    What is the typical lifespan of a Beagle?

    The average lifespan of a Beagle is 12-15 years.

    What are some common health issues for Beagles?

    Beagles can be prone to epilepsy, hypothyroidism, and ear infections.

    What is the temperament of a Beagle?

    Beagles are friendly, outgoing, and social animals that enjoy companionship.
